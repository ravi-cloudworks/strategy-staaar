2. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR Article 6)

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide the STAAAR service you've subscribed to
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): Marketing emails, cookies (non-essential), newsletter - you can withdraw anytime
  • Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)): Service improvement, fraud prevention, security - balanced against your rights
  • Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): Compliance with tax, accounting, and legal requirements

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Payment Data: Billing address, payment method (processed by Stripe - we never see full card numbers)
  • Profile Data: Optional company name, job title, professional information
  • User Content: STAAARs you create, templates you use, insights you input
  • Communications: Support messages, feedback, survey responses

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, clicks, session recordings (anonymised)
  • Device Data: IP address (anonymised after 90 days), browser type, operating system, device ID
  • Location Data: Approximate location from IP address (country/city level only)
  • Cookies: Essential (authentication, security) and optional (analytics) - see Section 8

3.3 Special Category Data

We do NOT collect special category data (racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, sexual orientation) unless you voluntarily include it in content you create. If you do, you are the data controller for that content.

10. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for staff
  • Regular Backups: Encrypted, geographically distributed
  • Security Audits: Annual penetration testing, vulnerability scans
  • Incident Response: 72-hour breach notification to ICO as required
  • Staff Training: Regular data protection and security training

Despite our measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You're responsible for keeping your password secure.
