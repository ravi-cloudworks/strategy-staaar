Stop selling "strategy," "model," or "framework." Start solving their searches. Instead of saying: "I do brand positioning and consumer insights," say: "I help brands answer: Why is my campaign failing? Why are competitors stealing our customers? How do we sell to Gen-Z without sounding cringe?"
